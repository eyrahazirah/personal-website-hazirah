My Experience

Hello, my readers. For this website, I will share my experience as a UiTM student.

My name is Nur Hazirah Iwani and I will share about my experience as a UiTM Student.

Everyone aspires to continue their education at a higher level. It's the same for me. This is because a person with a high education is highly respected by others. In addition, the knowledge learned can also be shared with many people because not many are fortunate enough to continue their studies at the University level. Everything that happens can definitely be a sweet or bitter experience to be remembered until old age. Therefore, as a UiTM student, there are many sweet experiences that I can save as memories.

I am a person who has never been away from family members, since I was a UiTM student, I need to be independent because I need to be away from family members and live in a residential college provided by UiTM. The residential college provided is very comfortable and many facilities can be used. The experience I remember the most was learning new subjects that I had never learned while in school. It was a bit hard for me to adjust but all that had to be changed to be a good student. While in school I never knew what a national archive was, how to make a citation, how to edit a video and so on. But since becoming a UiTM student, I have been introduced to such learning.

After studying those subjects from semester one to semester five, I became more proficient with what the lecturers had taught. I am so thankful to have so many good lecturers and understand all the needs of the students. One of them is Madam Seri Intan. Thank you for teaching me in this IMD311 subject so that I clearly understand how to create a personal website.
